FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can the vacuum be used without water?
The vacuum cannot be used without water.
How much does delivery cost in United Kingdom?
Delivery in United Kingdom is free.
Where can I find the serial number on the vacuum cleaner?
The serial number is located on the back of the main unit.
How to set a timer for air purification?
Press the ON button and hold for 6 seconds until the first line of the triangle blinks. Without removing your finger from the button, wait another 6 seconds. And it will turn on for 30 minutes.
Can the vacuum cleaner collect water and liquids?
Yes.
